Our service

Rental

Rental for Showcooking, Street Food, events, parties, festivals, Catering support, private parties, concerts, sports events, marketing, brand promotion, etc.

We support delivery and pick-up from the event's location.

Quality

All our trailers and vans include HACCP, as well as a valid Hygiene Certificate for restaurant-related businesses. They are delivered clean and empty of any products (food, drinks, cleaning supplies, etc), as well as any consumables.

For the rental period, the renter is responsible for: hygiene; quality of products on sale; any damages caused to the trailer during the event.

Customization

The rental may or may not include support equipment: tables, chairs, wastebaskets, electrical extensions, cleaning supplies, gas, beer machine pressurized gas, etc.

It is possible to customize the trailer/van for each case, as long as the materials used can be easily removed, and the vehicle is returned without lasting traces of the customization.
